5

investment focus

ml /ai

Superior performance and better accuracy are just two of the many current challenges in ml/ai and teams that have made fundamental breakthroughs by putting AI at the heart of their philosophy, is what excite us.

data

software startups that utlise all benefits of data will drive the next decade and bring astonishing results.fostering industry agnostic data development plays a major role in empowering businesses to make better decisions, resulting in upskilling and general efficiency increase

ed tech

digitalised education is a new reality where people from around the world can get access to the highest quality education. digital products and educational platforms will be the new “norm” ensuring people’s competency to be driven further.

fintech

horizontal fintech, regTech & insureTech firms who are able to show core competency in driving change, re-innovate and solve major obstacles are needed. we like to see firms that have the potential to turn into multi-layered tech giants servicing all needs of their clients.

automation

we believe that certain enterprise automation solutions will become a de facto standard in organisations, helping the workforce to upskill and onboard more complex tasks. the pace of automation is accelerating and more business value is generated through smart technological solutions.

cybersecurity

with an increase of technological devices and general shift towards digitalisation, cyberSecurity is ever more important. threats have become more sophisticated with entire infrastructures being vulnerable. as we are creating more and more value from our data we start to carry larger risks, with data-fueled applications projected to generate $13 trillion worth of new economic activity by 2030.
